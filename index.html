About the Author

Introduction to Self-Help Books

Self-help books became especially meaningful to me during my journey as an international student. Moving to a new country on my own at 19 was both exciting and challenging, and there were many moments when I felt overwhelmed, uncertain, and far from everything familiar. During that time, self-help books gave me guidance, comfort, and motivation. They helped me build confidence, stay focused on my goals, and develop a stronger mindset while adapting to a completely new environment. For me, self-help books were more than just something to read — they became a source of encouragement and personal growth during one of the most important transitions of my life.


Benefits of reading self-help books

  • Increase clarity and focus
  • Open your mind to new approaches
  • Get out of your comfort zone
  • Allows you to avoid doomscrolling
  • You can apply what you learned into your personal life
  • Motivates you to have better habits
  • Rewires your brain by promoting neuroplasticity
  • Helps you reduce anxiety
